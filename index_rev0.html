Welcome to True Offset!

True offset is a nonprofit service that sells high quality, solar infrastructure based carbon credits to climate concerned businesses and individuals based on their monthly, lifetime, or trip carbon output.

The True Offset Way

Accurately Track Your Carbon Output via the Carbon Profile

The best way to reduce carbon output is to not released in the first place.

At True Offset, we believe that the first step in solving climate change is understanding our individual and business carbon outputs and making changes to decrease them. By accurately tracking your monthly output and treating True Offset as a voluntary tax, you or your business are incentivised to make changes that will save you money on your voluntary monthly true offset bill.

High Quality Carbon Credits

The current carbon credit market is rife with low quality carbon credits which often cause more harm than good.

Each True Offset project must satisfy the following criteria as defined by the Greenhouse Gas Management Institute and Stockholm Environmental Institute:

  • Additional
  • Not Overestimated
  • Permanent
  • Not Claimed by Another Entity
  • Not Assosciated with Significant Social or Environmental Harms

Through the creation of new and permanent solar and battery infrastructure, True Offset strives to provide, well, true offsets!

Additional

Each True Offset Project creats new after the meter solar and battery infrastructure that is financed via your contributions, these projects cannot exist without your donations.

Not Overestimated

Each project's offset is calculated by dividing the total cost of the project by the expected lifetime output and the present and future conditions of the local grid.

Permanent

Each True Offset Project will be planned from conception to decommissioning and replacement of the old infrastructure.

Not Claimed by Another Entity

Each True Offset carbon credit is certified to be yours only. Double issueance, use, and claiming are strictly prohibited.

Not assosciated with Significant Social or Environmental Harms

All infrastructure providors and contractors must be vetted to ensure no assosciation with signifiant social or environmental harms.
